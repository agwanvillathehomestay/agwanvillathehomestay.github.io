About Us

Enjoy the elegance of a Beautiful and Luxurious stay in this Art Decor Home. Take an early morning stroll and enjoy the Beauty of Nature, dew filled garden. Moment the curtain rises or doors open, it milleus the imagination in a reality, chirping birds, scenic beauty with alluring Tree House.
Essentially, we offer you two double bedded rooms with its own attached bathrooms one of which overlooks the huge lavish hall while the other one is modernly done with an attached small fountain area which sets the romance high.The best and most creative part is the air-conditioned tree house which has a sofa cum bed well equipped with TV and music system with attached bathroom and yes there is a telescope kept to add on to the magical experience. In case if you want to have a camp adventure we do have a camping tent along with an outdoor fireplace. There is a comfortable hall with a fireplace, TV, Sofas and a dining table with a direct access to modular kitchen equipped with essential electronics like Refrigerator, Juicer etc There is a lavish green garden with a Gazebo, open-air theater, and a garden Jacuzzi which will give you a royal soothing experience.,but operable only in summers We also have a puppy house in case you want to be accompanied by your pet. One can also enjoy food prepared by locals which is cooked in traditional style on request and additional charges.
The villa was lovingly built with Marble stone floors, high beamed ceilings and antiques. It is located at Mansarovar, Patrakar Road, Jaipur, one of Asia's largest locality. Although shops and restaurants are only 5 to 10 minutes drive away, the area feels peaceful and secluded.
The major attraction is the Treehouse, and privacy of the stay where one can relax peacefully in the nature's lap. Let the villa be an excuse to visit your destination that calls you away from a hustled bustled city life.

Note :Homestay is available for occupancy and moderate gatherings but charges differ. Let us know in case we have left anything....we will try to arrange it for you.
